College of Information Technology

CIT College Ranchi, established in 1999, is one of Jharkhand’s premier technical institutions. We are committed to providing world-class education in engineering and information technology.

Our state-of-the-art campus spreads across 50 acres with modern laboratories, workshops, and facilities. We focus on practical learning and industry-relevant skills development.

Admission Process

Step 1: Online Registration

Visit our official website and fill out the online application form with valid details.

Step 2: Entrance Examination

Appear in the CIT Entrance Test or submit your JEE Main/Diploma scores for evaluation.

Step 3: Counseling & Document Verification

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for document verification and seat selection.

Step 4: Admission Confirmation

Pay the fees online or at the campus to confirm your admission.
